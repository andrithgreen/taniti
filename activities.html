Things to Do

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. However, there are plenty of other activities to keep you busy:

  • Local history museum
  • Chartered fishing tours
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest
  • Several pubs, including a microbrewery
  • Dancing at a new dance club
  • Movie theater
  • Helicopter rides
  • Arcade
  • Art galleries
  • Bowling
  • Nine-hole golf course (opening next year)

Merriton Landing: Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Sightseeing

Most tourists spend most of their time in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Other Popular Activities

  • Boat or bus tours of the island
  • Hikes in the rainforest
  • Visits to Taniti's active volcano
