Lodging on Taniti

Taniti has a wide variety of lodgings that range from an inexpensive hostel to one large, four-star resort. There are many small, family-owned hotels and a growing number of bed and breakfasts.

Good to know: All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government, so you can book with confidence regardless of your budget.
